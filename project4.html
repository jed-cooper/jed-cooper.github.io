DATA ANALYSIS
PROJECT 4

SUMMARY

This data project is an analysis of sales data for a company called Global Superstore.

An interactive dashboard was designed using Tableau Public, which brings the data to life by allowing stakeholders to explore sales data whilst filtering results to suit their needs.

Key Insights:
1. Highest monthly sales were recorded for the months of June, August and December.
2. Lowest monthly sales were recorded for the months of March, April and July.
3. Technology was the product category which contributed the most to overall sales.
4. Top five countries with the highest number of technological sales were Australia, China, India, Germany and France.
5. Sales of individual products amounting to less than 4000 may incur a loss in profits.

EXTENSIONS

The project could be extended to include:
- a time series analysis to identify any sales trends or seasonality.
- an analysis of sales by sub-category, identifying what products the company should focus on for larger profits and smaller losses.
