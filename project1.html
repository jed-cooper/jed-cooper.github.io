DATA ANALYSIS
PROJECT 1

SUMMARY

This data project explores the effectiveness and safety of a new drug used to treat type-2 diabetes. The data is derived from a Phase 3 clinical trial conducted over a 24-month period, which includes data from 200 participants.

Key Insights:
1. T-test indicates a significant difference in HbA1c blood concentration between the two groups.
2. Both the placebo and treatment groups experience significant reductions in HbA1c over time.
3. The treatment group shows significantly greater decreases in HbA1c at each time point compared to the placebo group (the treatment group experiences an additional 1.686-unit reduction in HbA1c at 24 months).
4. Chi-square test indicates no significant difference in the rate of adverse events experienced by either group.
